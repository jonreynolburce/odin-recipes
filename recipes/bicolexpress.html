Spicy and Creamy Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a spicy, creamy, and flavorful Filipino dish made with pork cooked in coconut milk and lots of chili peppers.

It’s inspired by the bold flavors of the Bicol region, known for its love of spicy food and coconut-based dishes. This recipe packs heat and richness in every bite

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g pork belly, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 can coconut milk (400ml)
  • 1 can coconut cream (optional, for thicker sauce)
  • 3–5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 5–10 green chilies (siling haba), sliced diagonally
  • 2–3 red chilies (siling labuyo), chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
