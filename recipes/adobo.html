Famous Chicken Adobo Recipe

Filipino Adobo is a classic Pinoy comfort food that everyone loves. It is usually made with chicken or pork, slowly cooked in a mix of soy sauce, vinegar.

Every family has their own version of adobo, and that is what makes it special. Whether it is a bit sweeter, more garlicky, or even with a twist like adding coconut milk, adobo is always a crowd favorite and a true taste of home.
